HOW TO PLAY
1
Choose Movies or TV Shows — all connections must stay in that category.
2
Search an actor, pick from the dropdown, then search the connecting movie/show below them.
3
Example: Leonardo DiCaprio → Wolf of Wall Street → Jonah Hill → 21 Jump Street → Channing Tatum
4
Pick a difficulty:
Easy — Each actor & title validates live as you go.
Medium — Fill the whole chain first, then tap Submit. Wrong connections are named.
Hard — Fill the whole chain first, then tap Submit. Only told "Incorrect" — no hints!
5
No repeats — the same actor or title can't appear twice in the chain.
